Tomoil logo

The shield logo is the most recognizable element of the Tomoil corporate identity. This logo takes priority, and in case of doubt about logo usage, we recommend using this variant. The logo with the tagline 'Powering Performance' is used as a closing element when there is sufficient space, such as on the back of a brochure, business card, or trailer. The landscape logo should only be used in exceptional cases, as a secondary option to the shield logo when limited space compromises readability and visibility. It is suitable for wide applications.
