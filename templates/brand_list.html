Tomoil logo

The shield logo is the most recognizable element of the Eurol corporate identity. This logo takes priority, and in case of doubt about logo usage, we recommend using this variant. The logo with the tagline 'Powering Performance' is used as a closing element when there is sufficient space, such as on the back of a brochure, business card, or trailer. The landscape logo should only be used in exceptional cases, as a secondary option to the shield logo when limited space compromises readability and visibility. It is suitable for wide applications.

The S.R.V. (acronym of Schwingung, Reibung, Verschleiß namely Oscillating, Friction, Wear) measures the physical interactions between a lubricant and two specimens in a loaded contact in either rotational or linear oscillatory motion.

It determines the efficiency of lubricants and greases by measuring friction, anti-wear (AW) and extreme pressure (EP) properties simulating real metal to metal sliding contact.
